

a. Write a MySQL query to find those customers with their name and those
salesmen with their name and city who lives in the same city

SELECT sm.name, cm.cust_name FROM salesman sm, customer cm WHERE sm.city = cm.city

b. Write a MySQL query to display all the orders which values are greater
than the average order value for October 10, 2012.

SELECT * FROM orders WHERE purch_amt > (SELECT AVG(purch_amt) FROM orders WHERE ord_date = '2012-10-10');


c. Write a MySQL query to list the employee ID, name, salary, department
name of all the 'MANAGERS' and 'ANALYST' working in SYDNEY,
MELBOURNE with an experience more than 5 years without receiving the
commission and display the list in ascending order of location.

SELECT emp_id, name, sal, dept FROM employee WHERE designation IN ['MANAGERS', 'ANALYST'] AND work_loc IN ['SYDNEY', 'MELBOURNE'] AND exp > 5 AND commission = 0;
